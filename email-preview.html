One week in — a quick closing note

Hi Ryan,

This is the last onboarding email from me. After this, Loam Logger should quietly do its job in the background and stay out of your way.

Some riders dive in immediately. Others don’t touch anything until a creak, a squeal, or a service comes due. Both are normal — and honestly, the second one is the behavior I’m designing for.

3 settings that make it feel more “you”

If you have 60 seconds, these are the only knobs worth looking at:

• Notifications: how noisy (or quiet) Loam Logger should be.

• Service intervals: if your preferences differ from defaults.

• Connected accounts: connect Garmin/Strava if you haven’t yet.

Open settings
